How CivicSafe Works

Your Safety is Our Priority

Learn how we protect your identity while ensuring your report reaches the right authorities.

📝

Step 01

Submit Your Report

Fill out our form with as much detail as possible. No personal information is required. You can include photos, video, or documents if available.

🔒

Step 02

Encryption & Anonymization

Your report is immediately encrypted using military-grade protocols. All identifying metadata is stripped from submission, including IP and device information.
